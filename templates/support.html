Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Check your inbox for reset instructions
How long do refunds take?
1. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days
2. Allow 5-10 days for bank processing
3. Contact your bank if not received after 15 days
How to create a listing?
1. Click the "Create New Listing" button near the top middle of the page
2. Select a title for the item
3. Select a description and price for the item
4. Select an image for your item
5. Click the "Create Listing" button at the bottom of the form.
